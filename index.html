About Infinity Gym

Founded in 2025, INFINITY Gym is a modern fitness center located at 199/ Siyambalagoda, Polgasovita. Our state-of-the-art facility is equipped with a wide range of equipment, and our knowledgeable staff is committed to helping you on your fitness journey.
